Before going any further, be 100% certain you have backed up the seed to your current wallet!
Without it, any funds you have will become completely unrecoverable!

When you create a new wallet, a new RaiBlocks seed will be generated which can be used to create brand new accounts on the RaiBlocks network. Your RaiBlocks seed is the master key to all of your accounts and by proxy any money inside of them!

Make sure you back up your seed, write it down, and keep it incredibly safe! There is NO way to recover it after creation, and all funds inside WILL be lost without it! You have been warned!

If you already have a RaiBlocks wallet, you can use your backup seed to import it below. When you import an existing wallet, we create a new wallet and attach your seed to it. None of your existing wallets or accounts are affected, and your seed is not stored anywhere besides in your local client.

After importing your wallet, you will need to 're-create' your accounts. Don't worry - all of your accounts are the same and your funds have not disappeared. Just go to the accounts section and press create new account to load your existing accounts.

Your new RaiBlocks wallet has been created! Make sure to write down/save your seed as it will be completely unrecoverable after you exit this screen!!
